Emily covered the 2016 presidential campaign, and subsequently worked as a White House correspondent for the international news agency Thomson Reuters.

She produced a range of stories, including:

  • Healthcare scramble writes new chapter in Trump's 'Art of the Deal'
  • Trump tests dealmaker image to sell healthcare bill

  • How Ted Cruz win in Supreme Court hurt U.S.-Mexico relations

Banking and Other Administrative

Prior to her campaign coverage, Emily covered banking for Reuters, as well as other bodies with administrative oversight, including the US Post Office. Her banking coverage specialized in Dodd-Frank implementation and Too-Big-To-Fail negotiations.
